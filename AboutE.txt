Function graphic
   Project made by the team formed by Munteanu
Catalin and Pasat Cosmin during the course
'Introduction to programming'.

The program has the following features:

- drawing the graph of any mathematical function
  corresponding to an interval in the system
  xoy coordinates including ramificated ones
- allows the zooming in and out effect
- displays the min / max points
- displays asymptotes
- detects the points of intersection with the axes
  of coordinates
- saves the graphic in the form of an image
  in BMP format
